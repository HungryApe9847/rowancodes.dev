    Slime Survivors!

    Controls are as follows:

    For PC:

  • WASD to move;
  • Arrow Keys to aim;
  • Space to shoot.

    • For Controller:

  • Left Stick to move;
  • Right Stick to aim;
  • Right Trigger to shoot.

    • Mobile coming soon!

    Age Rating thing.

    Oh, and there is no gore, blood, etc. in this game but there is an (unrealistic) pistol, for anybody that needs to know. So pretty much all ages here.
